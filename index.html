What Can You Find Here?

Here you can devour some of the most beautiful Olympiad-style problems which are really interesting to solve. I'm a high schooler who is obssesed with olympiads. I post blogs on different categories potraying my views on some topics/problems. My solutions are going to be crude as it's a documentary of working towards the solution. You may find incorrect conjectures or claims in my blogs which I later on correct it. I do this so that the problem can be seen from a birds-eye view which will help you understand the incorrect paths taken.

"Beginners are better teachers than that of the proffesionals,
because pros often forget that they were once a beginner themselves."

I even like to discuss on topics like philosophy and paradoxes and stuff. So you can expect those out here. I also also love reading novels especially the math ones,philosophy,biography etc. But haven't read any fiction, maybe in future that will be on my list. This means that you can also find book reviews and recommendations. Other than novels I would love to speak on some mathematics books which I have tried out and are phenomenal!
